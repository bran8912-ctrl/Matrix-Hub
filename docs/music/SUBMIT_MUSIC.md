# Music Submission Guide
Matrix-Hub.org

All music must comply with licensing and attribution rules.

---

- Artist Name:

- Source URL:

---

## File Requirements

- Format: MP3
- Quality: 192kbps or higher
- Filename: lowercase-with-dashes.mp3

---

## Attribution

You must update:
- `public/music/CREDITS.txt`
OR
- `public/music/LICENSE.md`

No attribution = no merge.

---

## Review Notes

Matrix Hub reserves the right to reject:
- Incompatible licenses
- Poor audio quality
- Off-theme music

Signal only.
